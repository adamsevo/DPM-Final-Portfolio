Vehicle Page

1991 Mazda Miata

2-Seat Roadster

This car is my main project, and my pride and joy. I purchased this car during my Senior year at Waukee from the lady that has lived behind me for all of my life. It was meticulously maintained, and was garage-stored and has never seen winter, which explains how there's no rust anywhere on the vehicle.

The weekend I bought it, I rolled it over to 60,000 miles. I made a summer full of memories in it going into my Freshman year here at Iowa, and it's made itself known as the car I'll never sell.

During my ownership, I have done the following:

  • New wheels & tires (15x7 +38 Enkei92s wrapped in G-Force Sport C2s)
  • ISR full catback exhaust
  • REV9 12-way adjustable coilovers
  • Halo LED Headlights
  • **Replaced engine with a '93 LNC 1.6L after short nose crank pulley failed**
  • Govee Sequential Underglow kit
  • JVC KW-X840BTS Head Unit
  • Kenwood KFC-X173 Excelon door speakers
  • Kicker 46CSC354 Headrest Speakers
