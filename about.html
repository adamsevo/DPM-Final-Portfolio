Throughout my childhood, I was always drawn to cars, which have remained my largest passion, interest, and hobby to this day. I love talking about, looking at, riding in, and driving fun vehicles of any kind, but my heart lies with 90s Japanese Domestic Market sports cars.

I've been able to leverage this passion during my time here in Iowa City by working at University Scooters as a moped technician, where I service a wide variety of mopeds and motorcycles. This has sparked my interests and hobbies with mopeds and motorcycles as well.

I also grew up around music, starting piano lessons in first grade and picking up the drums in middle school and high school jazz bands, show choirs, and jazz choirs. Into college, I have continued to drum and play the cajon for my church every week.

Technology and engineering have always been in my veins. Growing up working with circuits and piecing together computer equipment as a child has refined my interests in IT and anything technology-related, which I have made the main focus of my higher education here at the University of Iowa as I'm studying Business Analytics and Information Systems. My upcoming role with EY in Des Moines will allow me to continue down this path of leveraging my experience and passion for IT!
